Pediatrician

A pediatrician is a medical doctor who treats children. They provide: Preventive health maintenance and ongoing monitoring for healthy children. Medical care for children who are acutely or chronically ill.

Our Specialist Paediatricians take pride in providing quality specialist care to children from birth until 18 years of age. They have over 20 years of experience in managing child health conditions including:

  • Learning Difficulties
  • Newborn conditions
  • Allergy and Eczema
  • Bedwetting and Voiding Dysfunction
  • Seizures disorders
